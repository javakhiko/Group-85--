What Is Human?
A human is a unique living being who belongs to the species Homo sapiens. Humans differ from other animals through their advanced thinking, self-awareness, and ability to create complex societies. Their brains allow them to learn, imagine, plan for the future, and solve difficult problems. This intelligence is the foundation for science, culture, technology, and communication. Humans are also emotional creatures. They feel happiness, sadness, fear, love, and empathy. These emotions help them form relationships, cooperate with others, and build communities. At the same time, humans often struggle with conflict, misunderstanding, and the challenge of balancing personal desires with social values. What makes humans truly special is their search for meaning. People ask who they are, why they exist, and how they should live. Through art, philosophy, religion, and exploration, humans constantly try to understand themselves and the world around them.
